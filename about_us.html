About Us

Limerencia Films is a Mexican independent film production company founded in Mexico by Lila Avilés with the film "The Chambermaid" which had a great reception by critics and several international awards. Limerencia Films intends to consolidate itself as a production company that respects with total freedom each of the creative processes of the directors, from the first seed to all the cycles that unite a movie looking for a diverse audience. Limerencia Films will continue producing author films with deep and risky themes, looking for new cinematographic narratives and with international projection. It is also interested, to look for projects in different languages and with difficult access to consolidate a narrower bridge between the artist and the public.

Lila Avilés

Lila Avilés is a Mexican film director, screenwriter and producer recognized for her feature film "The Chambermaid". Her beginnings began in theater working in more than twenty staging and then moved to the film direction. She founded the cinema company LIMERENCIA FILMS focusing on the direction and production of authorial cinema. "THE CHAMBERMAID" premiered in 2018 at the TIFF and in Europe at the San Sebastian Festival of the same year. Her film won a large number of international and national awards and a long journey at the world's leading film festivals, as well as distribution in many countries.
