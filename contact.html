Limerencia Films

is a Mexican independent film production company founded in Mexico by Lila Avilés with the film "The Chambermaid" which had a great reception by critics and several international awards. Limerencia Films intends to consolidate itself as a production company that respects with total freedom each of the creative processes of the directors, from the first seed to all the cycles that unite a movie looking for a diverse audience. Limerencia Films will continue producing author films with deep and risky themes, looking for new cinematographic narratives and with international projection. It is also interested, to look for projects in different languages and with difficult access to consolidate a narrower bridge between the artist and the public.
